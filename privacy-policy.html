Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Our "No-Data" Philosophy

At Nodeworx, we build "Privacy-First" utilities. Whether you are tracking stocks or documenting your home, we believe your data belongs to you. We do not use third-party tracking pixels, we do not sell your data, and we do not create "user profiles" to track your behavior.

Product-specific Data Handling

Product What we collect Where it is stored Retention
TickerTracker Stock symbols & preferences. Your Browser (LocalStorage) Until you clear your browser data.
ClaimReady Email, Item names, & Photos. Secure Cloud (Temporary) Deleted after 24 hours.
  • TickerTracker Details: We never see your portfolio or watchlists. All data lives exclusively on your device. If you switch computers or clear your cache, your data will be reset.
  • ClaimReady Details: We collect your email only to deliver your PDF. Your photos are used strictly to generate your report and are purged from our servers automatically to ensure your privacy.

What We Collect

When you use ClaimReady, we temporarily collect the following information:

  • Email address: Used only to send your finished PDF and to help resume an interrupted session.
  • Inventory data: Item names, values, and photos are stored temporarily while your report is built.
  • Payment info: We never see or store credit card details — payments are processed by Polar.

How We Store and Delete Data

When you use ClaimReady, we store your data securely and delete it automatically:

Images: Photos are uploaded to a secure, private storage bucket and are automatically deleted 24 hours after upload.

No accounts: We do not create persistent user profiles. Once your session expires or your report is delivered, we keep no permanent record of your home's contents.

No trackers: We do not use advertising trackers, pixels, or third-party analytics that follow you across the web.

Third-Party Services

We use a small set of trusted partners to provide functionality:

  • Cloudflare — temporary image storage, hosting
  • Polar — secure payment processing
  • Postmark — to deliver the final PDF via email

Your Rights

When using ClaimReady, because we do not retain your data long-term, there is generally nothing to export or delete. TickerTracker stores data on your device, so you can manage it through your browser settings.
